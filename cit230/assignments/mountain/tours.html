Tours

We couldn't be more thrilled to share our passion for cycling with you! Whether you're looking for a leisurely ride near the rivers, or a challenging ride through the canyons, we've got you covered.

Main Street and Beyond!

Starting at our shop on Valley Commons Drive, one of our local guides will take you through town to the most historic buildings and parks. The ride lasts for about 2 hours, with 3 breaks at 30 minute intervals

  • Price: $15
  • Level: Easy
  • Bike Type: Road Bike Preferred

Winding Rivers

Starting at the Hazel Creek Trailhead, this unique adventure takes riders through looming ponderosa pines and fragrant douglas firs. Although the terrain is a little bumpy, this ride is great for spotting native wildlife.

  • Price: $15
  • Level: Moderate
  • Bike Type: Mountain Bike
