Welcome!

My name is David Laws, and this is a compilation of my interests, school projects, and the beauty I find in life. I hope as you explore my photography and listen to my stories you will find curiosity and gratitude in your heart. The beauty we find in simplicity will outweigh any riches in this life.

Perhaps John Muir expressed it best when he wrote, "Everybody needs beauty as well as bread, places to play in and pray in, where nature may heal and give strength to body and soul."
